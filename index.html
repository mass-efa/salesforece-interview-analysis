üêª Bear Case: Seat Compression

  • Piper Sandler: Lowered price target due to "Seat Compression" concerns. If AI agents do the work of 10 people, companies will buy fewer Salesforce "seats."

  • Transition Risk: Stock under pressure as investors debate whether the shift from seats to agents helps or hurts long-term.
